The Future is Interdisciplinary

At STRIDE, we believe the most impactful innovations happen at the intersection of disciplines. Our programs encourage collaboration across engineering domains, combining expertise to solve complex, real-world problems.

Whether you're interested in AI-powered medical devices, sustainable smart cities, or robotics for disaster relief, STRIDE provides the platform to explore, learn, and create across boundaries.
