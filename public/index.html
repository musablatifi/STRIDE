Who We Are

STRIDE is an NGO formed by Engineering Students & Young Professionals to Empower Self and Society through Supportive Community of Engineers and Industry experts.

We are a student-centered platform built to uplift learners in the fields of emerging technologies, innovation, entrepreneurship, and advanced soft and hard skills.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to empower Students and Young professionals by providing Trustworthy Mentorship, Fostering Skills and nurturing Talent through Supportive Community of Engineers and Industry experts.

What Drives Us

Our core objectives guide everything we do, from mentorship to innovation

Community Building

Promote lifetime contribution through continuous learning and mentorship

Career Growth

Facilitate access to opportunities, jobs, and higher education

Emerging Technologies

Upskill in cutting-edge technologies with ethical practices

Research & Innovation

Foster culture of research aligned with societal values

Networking & Mentorship

Connect students with professionals and industry experts

Values-Driven Education

Promote moral values in technical education

Talent Development

Raise awareness and support entrepreneurial aspirations
